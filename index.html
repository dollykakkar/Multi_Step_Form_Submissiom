WELCOME TO AIEESE (Secondary) - 2025

ALL INDIA ENGINEERING ENTRANCE SCHOLARSHIP EXAMINATION-SECONDARY (AIEESE-Secondary) is an examination body that offers scholarships to aspiring students who qualify for government engineering colleges, including IITs, NITs, and IIITs, through the JEE examinations.

It is crucial for all aspirants to take the AIEESE (Secondary) exam because if they secure admission to IITs, NITs, or IIITs, they become eligible to receive a scholarship. Scholarships are awarded based on their performance in the AIEESE (Secondary) examination.

AIEESE (Secondary) was established in 2012 under the "Scholarship and Admission Eligibility Testing Agency (SAET Agency)." Since then, the examination has been conducted annually. The objective of AIEESE (Secondary) is to provide financial assistance to engineering aspirants who qualify for IIT, NIT, or IIIT institutions based on their performance in the AIEESE (Secondary) exam. Students can benefit from scholarships covering up to four years of tuition fees.

Since 2012, hundreds of thousands of students from all across India have applied for and benefited from this scholarship. Most of the students who qualify for IITs, NITs, and IIITs have applied for the AIEESE (Secondary) scholarship program to take advantage of the financial benefits. The students can avail up to the complete course fee as a scholarship based on the merit list prepared through the AIEESE Secondary Examination. Read More
